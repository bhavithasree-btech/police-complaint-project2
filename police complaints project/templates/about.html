About ReportSafe

Many students and youth hesitate to report issues such as bullying, harassment, and unsafe environments due to fear of retaliation, lack of awareness, or complex reporting systems. ReportSafe is built to simplify this process and protect student safety.

Our Safety Reporting Protocols

  • Anonymous Submissions: When filing anonymously, your email, name, and contact details are completely hidden. The system generates a single token key for you to track updates.
  • Encrypted Storage: Sensitive details and attachments are secured to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Fast Review Action: Administrators review submissions immediately and route them to relevant authorities (school heads, civic bodies, or counselors).

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can submit a report?

Students, parents, and youth aged 13-25 who are facing bullying, harassment, safety issues at school/college, or civic issues in their neighborhoods.

What happens after I submit a complaint?

You will receive a unique tracking ID. An admin will check your complaint, mark it 'Under Review', contact relevant stakeholders, and keep you updated on the progress.

Is my data completely secure?

Yes, we utilize top security hashes and standards to guard database integrity and prioritize data privacy.
